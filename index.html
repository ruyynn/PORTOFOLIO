01 / About

The Mind Behind the Terminal

I'm Natanael, known online as Ruyynn — a self-driven security researcher from Indonesia with a deep obsession for finding what others overlook.

My journey into cybersecurity wasn't a straight path. It started with curiosity — wanting to understand how systems work underneath the surface. That curiosity evolved into a methodology: structured reconnaissance, systematic enumeration, and rigorous responsible disclosure.

I don't just scan and report. I think like an attacker to defend like a professional. Every vulnerability I find is handled through proper disclosure channels — because the goal is always to make the internet safer, not to cause harm.

Recognized by multiple CSIRT institutions in Indonesia for responsible disclosure contributions, I'm committed to making real-world impact through ethical security research.

I am committed to responsible disclosure in every finding and will never misuse any discovered access.
